Q&A Q&A

What is your typical project timeline?

Most end-to-end product design projects take between 4-8 weeks. Design system projects vary based on complexity but typically start at 6 weeks for a comprehensive v1 release.

Do you handle development?

My core expertise is design. However, I am proficient in HTML/CSS and Webflow. For complex web apps, I partner with trusted engineering teams or work alongside your developers.

How do you handle revisions?

I work in weekly sprints with frequent check-ins (Async or Sync), which minimizes the need for massive "revisions" at the end. Feedback is incorporated continuously.
