About Me About Me

I am Nischhal Raj Subba, a Product Designer with 6+ years of experience crafting digital ecosystems. Ranked #1 globally by Uxcel, I specialize in bridging the gap between complex requirements and elegant, scalable user interfaces.

The Person Behind the Pixels

Design, for me, is the intersection of logic and empathy. My journey has taken me from crafting intuitive mobile apps to architecting massive enterprise platforms and decentralized applications (Web3). I believe that a robust Design System is the foundation of creativity, not a constraint.

My philosophy is rooted in Atomic Design and System Thinking. I don't just design screens; I build living systems that empower engineering teams to move faster and ensure products scale gracefully. I thrive in environments where data density is high, and the problem space is ambiguous.

Whether it's simplifying a complex fintech dashboard or designing a trustless interaction for a blockchain wallet, my goal remains the same: Make it intuitive, make it beautiful, make it work.
