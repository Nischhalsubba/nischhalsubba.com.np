I'm a Product Designer based in Kathmandu, bridging the gap between design vision and engineering reality.

My Story

I started my journey exploring how things work on the web. What began as simple HTML experiments evolved into a deep passion for large-scale product design.

Over the last 5 years, I've focused on the intersection of design and code. I believe the best products are built when designers understand the medium they are designing for.

Currently, I'm specializing in Fintech and Design Systems, helping teams move faster by creating consistent, scalable, and accessible component libraries.

Values

Clarity over Cleverness

I prioritize intuitive, standard patterns over flashy interactions that confuse users. Good design is invisible.

Systems Thinking

I don't design pages; I design systems. Every component is built to be reused, scaled, and maintained.

Inclusive by Default

Accessibility isn't a checklist item; it's the baseline. I ensure my designs work for everyone, everywhere.

Radical Transparency

I share work early and often. Feedback loops are the engine of improvement, not a hurdle.
