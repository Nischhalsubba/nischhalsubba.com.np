My Story My Story

I am a Product Designer based in Kathmandu, Nepal, with over 5 years of experience in crafting digital products. I specialize in bridging the gap between design vision and engineering reality, ensuring that what we design is scalable, accessible, and performant.

My journey began with front-end development, which gives me a unique perspective on design constraints and feasibility. I realized that the most frustrating part of development was often ambiguous design. I decided to move upstream to fix that.

Today, I lead design initiatives for Fintech and Enterprise SaaS companies, helping them simplify complex workflows into intuitive experiences. I believe in "systems over screens" — thinking about how a component lives, scales, and breaks before it ever hits the canvas.
