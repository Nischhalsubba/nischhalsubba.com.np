Travel Platform Marketplace UX 2022

Jeweltrek Jeweltrek

Home of the Himalayas and the birthplace of the legendary Buddha, Nepal is an awe-inspiring destination for every traveler. Jeweltrek brings that experience online with a trekking discovery and booking platform focused on itinerary design, trust cues, and content hierarchy.

View Figma Prototype
Role

Senior UI/UX Designer
UX, UI, Content Strategy

Team

PM, 2 Engineers, 1 QA

Timeline

Nov 2021 - Jan 2023

Outcome

Clear trek discovery and booking flow

THE PROBLEM

Travelers could not compare treks or trust local operators.

The market was fragmented, with little clarity on trek difficulty, best seasons, or operator credibility. Users needed a fast way to evaluate options without reading pages of copy.

"Confidence comes from detail, but only if the detail is easy to scan."

Goals & Success Metrics

Goal
Discovery Speed

Help travelers find the right trek in minutes.

Goal
Trust Signals

Make guide quality and safety visible on every listing.

Goal
Booking Confidence

Clarify pricing, inclusions, and itinerary details.

FIGMA PROTOTYPE

Marketplace UI and itinerary storytelling

The Figma file captures the full discovery journey, from trek browsing to itinerary detail and booking confidence. Visual hierarchy was tuned for scanability without losing the romance of the Himalayas.

DESIGN SYSTEM

Content-first marketplace UI

Key Components

A modular card system highlights trek difficulty, seasonality, and credibility without overwhelming users.

  • Trek cards with difficulty and altitude indicators
  • Itinerary timeline with day-by-day highlights
  • Operator badges and verified reviews
Difficulty: Moderate | Duration: 10 Days | Altitude: 5,416m

Final Implementation

Delivered a responsive marketplace with improved discovery, clearer itinerary pages, and booking-ready UX.
