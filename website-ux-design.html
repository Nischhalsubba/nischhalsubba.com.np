Website UX areas I focus on

Content structure

Make the business easier to understand

Clarify services, page hierarchy, proof points, process, and user questions so visitors do not have to guess.

SEO UX

Design pages that search engines and people can read

Use focused headings, internal links, descriptive sections, and page-specific copy that supports search intent.

Conversion UX

Guide visitors toward the right action

Shape CTAs, inquiry flows, segmented lead paths, and contact prompts around visitor intent.
