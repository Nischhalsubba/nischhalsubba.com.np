Neverwinter Live Parser

An ongoing Windows-first desktop app for reading Neverwinter combat logs in real time and turning raw encounter data into readable performance insights for players, parties, dungeons, trials, and recorded log files.

Role

Product Designer / Builder

Stack

Electron, React, TypeScript, Vite, Recharts

Focus

Live parsing, session review, data clarity

Why I am building it

Neverwinter combat logs contain useful information, but raw logs are difficult to read during actual gameplay. I wanted a local-first tool that could help players understand damage, healing, support, target focus, encounter timing, and session history without forcing everything into a confusing spreadsheet.

What I worked on

  • Windows-first desktop app direction and local-first workflow.
  • Live combat log tracking and recorded log review requirements.
  • Encounter, session, party, damage, healing, support, and target analysis structure.
  • Readable chart direction using tables for comparison and graphs for patterns.
  • Parser quality principles around correctness, uncertainty, and diagnostics.

This is an ongoing personal product. The case study is framed as active work, not a finished commercial product.
