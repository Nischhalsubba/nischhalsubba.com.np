Mokshya.io

I designed the Mokshya.io website to communicate a Web3 protocol and developer-focused product in a clearer, more trustworthy way. The work focused on hierarchy, visual direction, product storytelling, and responsive website UX.

THE DESIGN PROBLEM

Technical Web3 ideas needed a clearer public website experience.

The website needed to explain what Mokshya is, why it matters, and how the product connects to wallet, protocol, and developer-facing use cases. My thinking was to reduce jargon where possible, create a clearer page hierarchy, and make the experience feel trustworthy before asking users or developers to explore deeper.

What I worked on

  • Homepage structure and visual direction.
  • Web3 product messaging and section hierarchy.
  • Developer/product storytelling for technical audiences.
  • Trust-focused landing page sections.
  • Responsive website layout direction.
