Nova Financial

Redefining the mobile banking experience for a new generation of investors.

Role

Lead Product Designer

Timeline

3 Months

Client

Nova Corp

The Challenge

Nova Financial wanted to expand their user base to include Gen Z investors. However, their legacy application was cluttered, slow, and full of financial jargon that intimidated new users. The goal was to simplify the interface without losing advanced functionality.

My Approach

  • Conducted user interviews with 15 participants aged 18-25.
  • Audit of competitors (Robinhood, Revolut, Monzo).
  • Created low-fidelity wireframes focusing on "One action per screen".
  • Developed a dark-mode first UI system to align with modern trends.

The Solution

I designed a dashboard that prioritizes net worth visualization and quick actions. We moved complex trading tools to a secondary layer, keeping the primary interface clean. We also introduced "Nova Academy", a gamified learning hub.
